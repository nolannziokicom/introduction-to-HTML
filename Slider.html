Endangered animals

Image 1 is a snow leopard;
they live in harsh steep mountain regions
they are endangered because of human wildlife conflict and climate change


Image 2 is known as the pangolin;
pangolins live in shubby forests and areas with sandy soil
there diet is insects they are endangered because of their keratin scales and they are used for traditional medicine


The last image is a fluffy red bear known as a red panda;
red pandas live in high altitude mountains in the himalayas
they mostly eat bamboo
they are endangered because of their red fur and also due to deforestation which destroys there homes
